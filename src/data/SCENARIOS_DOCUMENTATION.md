# Scenarios Documentation

Scenarios allow users to compare different economic and operational futures for the asset.

## 1. Base Case (Default)
- **Description**: Standard business-as-usual forecast.
- **Oil Price**: $75/bbl (Flat)
- **Gas Price**: $3.50/mcf (Flat)
- **Discount Rate**: 10%
- **Outcome**: Reference EUR and NPV.

## 2. High Price Environment
- **Description**: Optimistic market conditions.
- **Oil Price**: $90/bbl
- **Gas Price**: $4.50/mcf
- **Outcome**: Increased economic limit life, higher reserves, significantly higher NPV.

## 3. Low Price Environment (Stress Test)
- **Description**: Conservative downside case.
- **Oil Price**: $50/bbl
- **Gas Price**: $2.50/mcf
- **Outcome**: Reduced economic life, lower reserves, potential negative NPV on marginal wells.

## 4. Artificial Lift Uplift
- **Description**: Operational enhancement scenario.
- **Modifier**: +15% Rate uplift applied to forecast.
- **Cost**: +10% LOE increase (lifting costs).
- **Outcome**: Accelerated recovery, higher near-term cash flow.

## 5. Facility Constraint
- **Description**: Infrastructure bottleneck.
- **Constraint**: Maximum liquid rate capped at 2,000 bbl/d for the group.
- **Outcome**: production deferral, lower near-term NPV, unchanged EUR.
